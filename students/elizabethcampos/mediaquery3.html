Neggs are special neopian treats which neopets love. Neggs are a good way to start collections. You may be able to trade simple neggs for better neggs at the neggery located in the Ice Caves of Terror Mountain. At the neggery the negg faerie spends her time collecting and trading neggs. When you give her a negg you get tokens that you will collect to get better neggs in exchange.
